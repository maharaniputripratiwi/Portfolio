About Me

Hello I am Maharani Putri Pratiwi, a dedicated creative designer, photographer, and videographer. With a background in visual arts and professional experience, I focus on creating stunning and meaningful visual works. In both photography and videography, I strive to capture the best moments, bring design concepts to life, and create memorable visuals.

With a creative and detailed approach, I give a unique touch to each project to ensure a satisfying result that meets the client's needs. This portfolio showcases a variety of my work, ranging from portrait photography, graphic design, to documentary videos.
